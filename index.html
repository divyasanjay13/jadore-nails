About J'adore Nails

We're a proud mother–son team from Philadelphia who brought our passion for beauty and business to Cherry Hill in 2023. With over 20 years of salon experience, our family created J'ADORE NAILS as a fresh, modern space where self-care and community come first.

Step into our newly renovated salon and you'll find a relaxing, hygienic, and uplifting environment complete with thoughtful touches to make you feel at home. Our talented team of nail technicians (many from Philadelphia too!) are here to deliver everything from classic mani-pedis to bold, custom designs.

At J'ADORE NAILS, we love what we do — and we can't wait to help you look and feel your best.
